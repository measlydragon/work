Mum's Famous Vegetable Soup

Soup Even Non-soupies Will Love!

Need soup even soup haters will eat? Try this! If it doesn't work, just say: No soup for you!"

Ingredient List

1 box/cube Stock - chicken or vegetable

1 Leek

1/2 Large Onion

1 Large Carrot

1 stalk Celery

1 tbs Butter

Salt & Pepper to taste

Optional -chopped Parsley as garnish

  1. Using a food processor, or by hand if you don't have one, chop the leek, onion, carrot, and celery until the pieces are your desired texture. We recommend small pieces but not pureed.

  2. Take a large pot and turn the heat to medium-high. Add the butter and then add your chopped vegetables. No stock yet! now let the vegetables 'sweat' till they are warmed through. Go ahead, give them a stir here and there so they get evenly warmed and nice and mixed up!

  3. Now, add your chicken or vegetable stock, you can use a few cubes or boxes if you need to. If you prefer less stock, put in the desired amount and top-up with water.

  4. Next you will want to put the lid on it and bring it to a boil stirring occasionally. (Don't let it boil over!) Toss in a pinch or two of salt and pepper to taste, usually a bit more salt than pepper. Then turn the heat to low or medium-low and simmer it for a few hours. Don't forget to check on it once in awhile!

  5. Now make sure that it is to your liking, add more salt and/or pepper if you need to, and serve it up with a pinch of chopped parsley sprinkled in the centre of each bowl. YUM!!
